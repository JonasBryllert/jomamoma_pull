Welcome to JOMAMOMA games

Here you can play online classic games like Memory, X and O, by challenging someone else. You need to enter your name to continue.

Tell me more.

JOMAMOMA is a site for playing classic kids games over internet. You login with a user name of your liking. Make sure your opponents knows your user name so they can challenge you. If you enter a group name you will only be able to challenge (or be challenged) by people who join the same group, so this is a way of privacy. Agree with your friends a group name and challenge each other.

All games are for two players and are typical mind challenge games that are great to play with kids for example memory, X and O, sink ships etc.

A good idea is to keep a Skype or phone call open so you can chat and have fun while playing.
