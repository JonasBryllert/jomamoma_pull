JOMAMOMA - Sink ship

Welcome @userName @oppName @size
Need to know the rules? Click here.

The goal of the SinkShip game is to sink the opponents ship before he/she sinks yours. Below you have two tables with blue background depicting the sea.

In the right table you can see your own ships (grey background) and when it is the opponents turn you will see a X marking where he/she clicked and it will be red if it hit one of you ships. When the ship is sunk (all cells for the ship is clicked) the background color will turn red and a point is given in the score table.

When it is your turn you click on cell in the opponents table and you will be informed if hit or miss. If a hit the background color for the cell will turn grey to indicate that there is a ship in that position. If you sink a ship the background color will go red and you will be given a point in the score table.

There are 5 ships each, 2 with length 2, 2 with length 3, and 1 with length 5.
